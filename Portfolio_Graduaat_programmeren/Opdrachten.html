Project and POP-session


POP – reflection exercise

In this exercise we needed to reflect on a 2 situations in 2 different ways: STARR-method and Kolb.
The goal was to learn how to reflect on a situation, so you learn things out of them. My
experience in this exercise was that it was quite easy because I had to reflect on many situations in
my previous study. I just dusted off my reflection skills and looked at how the 2 methods on how
to use them properly.



POP – planning exercise

In this exercise we needed to make a planning of the next 2 weeks at that time. I learned about how
handy planning can be and how important it is to plan your day, so you have time too yourself
and not get consumed by school or work. The difficulties I had was finding stuff to add because I
am a person that does things on the fly instead of planning it.



POP – Exercise guest speaker

In this exercise we needed to talk about what we thought about the guest speaker. We had to answer
a few questions about what the guest speaker talked about and I learned about 1 of the massive
choice in the field of being a programmer. It’s quite interesting that there are workplaces that
are for testing programs and projects.

Groepsproject

We had to do a group project that lasted from 15th of February until the 30th of May. We had too
develop a front-end that is connected too a back-end coded with C# that had controllers that
would connect too your local database for several things like logging in, registering, making
orders, filling certain pages with all the products by getting all the data from the database. In our
case we made a renting service for retro consoles that had taken inspiration from retro arcade
with neon colours and a font that resembles text from and arcade machine.
